## 🤖 Overview
Rogo makes the hardest parts of internet research easier. When you have an open-ended question, you’re struggling to find good sources, or you’re looking for a tough-to-find metric, Rogo is the perfect place to turn.

## 🌐 Tracking down data points like market share (Web)
Metrics like market share, store count, volume, and others are typically spread out across the internet across a variety of sources. Rather than searching through a million websites, you can just use Rogo to do that work for you. Of course, it’s still up to you to decide which sources / numbers are trustworthy.
