## ✅ Account Creation

1. Go to [tryrogo.com](https://tryrogo.com/).
2. Hit “Log in” in the bottom left corner.
3. Hit “Continue with Google” or “Continue with Microsoft Account” depending on your provider.
4. If you don't have Microsoft or Google, or if you run into any issues, click "Sign up" below and create a username and password.
5. Sign in.
6. Email [john@rogodata.com](mailto:john@rogodata.com) if you run into any issues!
