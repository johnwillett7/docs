## 🤖 Overview
For every result you get back from Chat or Deep Research, you can export it into a sendable email or straight into PowerPoint.

Please contact us to create customized PowerPoint exports in your branding and templates.

## ⬆️ Exporting
1. Clicking “Email” will generate a sendable email with sources.
2. For PowerPoint export, choose if you want all the information on a single page or an an outline with a topic / point on each page.
